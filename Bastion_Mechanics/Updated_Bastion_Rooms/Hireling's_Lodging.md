# Hireling's Lodging
**Type** Synergy
**Initial Size** 6
**Max Size** no limit
**Cost to Build** 500 GP
**Time to Build** 1 turn
**Cost to Expand** 20 GP x current size
**Requirements** 2x3 square area
**Exclusive With** None
**Minimum Level** 5
**Hirelings** 0

## Perks
*Housed Hireling.* You can house 1 hireling.

*Spread out.* This room can be built across the bastion and doesn't need to connect to itself.
