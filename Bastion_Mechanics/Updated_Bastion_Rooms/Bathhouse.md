# Bathhouse
**Type** Trade
**Initial Size** 9
**Max Size** 17
**Cost to Build** 1000 GP
**Time to Build** 3 turns
**Cost to Expand** 150 GP
**Requirements** A 2x3 square area for the bathing area.
**Minimum Level** 9
**Hirelings** 1

## Perks
*Freshly Clean.* After taking a long bath in the bathhouse, you have a +3 to Charisma checks towards anyone who even slightly respects being clean. This effect disappears early if you get sufficiently dirty or bloody as decreed by the DM.

## Actions
*Open to the Public.* The bathhouse is opened to the public for the bastion turn. This earns you 2d6 x 50 GP, but you become unable to benefit from the *Freshly Clean* passive. This option requires you to be in or near a settlement of at least 100 people.

## Room Features
### Onsen
**Square Cost** 3
**Cost to Build** 5000 GP
**Time to Build** 3 turns
**Requirements** None
**Exclusive With** Spa
**Minimum Level** 13
**Hirelings Added** 1

#### Perks
*Fulfilling Relaxation.* Spending an hour in the Onsen reduces your exhaustion level by 1 and you gain temporary hit points equal to twice your level. This can only occur once per day.

*Unique Service.* Increase the dice rolled for *Open to the Public* by 2 stages. This increases to 3 stages at level 17.

#### Action
*Refreshed Beyond Compare.* By spending at least an hour in the onsen every day during the bastion turn, increase your constitution score and max by +2 for one bastion turn. This increases to +4 at level 17 and +5 at level 19.

### Spa
**Square Cost** 3
**Cost to Build** 5000 GP
**Time to Build** 3 turns
**Requirements** None
**Exclusive With** Onsen
**Minimum Level** 13
**Hirelings Added** 3

#### Perks
*Majestic Appearance.* Improves the effect of *Freshly Clean* to last 12 hours and increases the bonus by +2. It also becomes notably harder to get dirty enough to lose the effect.

*Special Service.* Increase the number of dice rolled for *Open to the Public* by +1. This increases to +2 at level 15, +3 at level 17 and +4 at level 19.

#### Action
*Bodily Infusion.* By spending at least an hour being tended to in the spa every day during the bastion turn, increase all of your saving throw modifiers by +1 for one bastion turn. This effect increases to +2 at level 17.

### Herb-Infusion Station
**Square Cost** 2
**Cost to Build** 3000 GP
**Time to Build** 1 turn
**Requirements** 1x2 square area
**Exclusive With** None
**Minimum Level** 11
**Hirelings Added** 1

#### Perks
*Revitalizing Water.* Spending half an hour in the bathhouse allows you to recover 50 HP or a quarter of your max, whichever is higher. This can only be done once per day. Additionally, increase the value gained by *Open to the Public* by +25 GP.

*Home Grown.* If you have the Garden room, double the the HP recovered by *Revitalizing Water* and reduce the time to 20 minutes. If your garden has the Magical Herb Garden feature, double the HP once more, increase the GP by another +25, and reduce the time to 10 minutes.


### Water Enchanter
**Square Cost** 2
**Cost to Build** 3000 GP
**Time to Build** 1 turn
**Requirements** 1x2 square area
**Exclusive With** None
**Minimum Level** 11
**Hirelings Added** 1

#### Perks
*Magical Water.* It takes half as long to receive any benefits from the bathhouse. 

*Improved Experience.* For *Open to the Public*, increase the dice rolled by 1, the dice stage by 1, and the GP earned by 10.

### Improved Public Service
**Square Cost** 4
**Cost to Build** 5000 GP
**Time to Build** 1 turn
**Requirements** 2 1x2 square areas
**Exclusive With** None
**Minimum Level** 11
**Hirelings Added** 2

#### Perks
*Improved Water Purification.* The water purification has been improved to the point where *Open to the Public* no longer disables *Freshly Clean*.

#### Action
*Mass Opening.* The bathhouse is opened to the public for the bastion turn with better marketing, but you become unable to benefit from the *Freshly Clean* passive. To calculate your earnings for this action, use the same dice and GP value as *Open to the Public*, but multiply the number of dice by 2x. This increases to 4x at level 15 and 8x at level 19.
