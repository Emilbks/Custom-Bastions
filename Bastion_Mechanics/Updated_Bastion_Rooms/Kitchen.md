# Kitchen
**Type** Enhancement
**Initial Size** 6
**Max Size** 12
**Cost to Build** 600 GP
**Time to Build** 2 turns
**Cost to Expand** 100 GP
**Requirements** None
**Minimum Level** 5
**Hirelings** 1

## Effects
### Passive
*Hearty Meal.* Eating a meal cooked in the kitchen grants you temp HP equal to your character level that last until you take a long rest. The food loses its effectiveness if not consumed within an hour.

### Actions
*Takeaway.* You order your hireling to cook and sell food from the kitchen to passerbys for the turn. This generates 2d6 x 20 GP over the course of the turn. Doing this takes up so much of the kitchen that there's no room for making a *Hearty Meal*. This option requires you to be in or near a settlement of at least 50 people.
